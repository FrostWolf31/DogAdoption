About Our Organization

Here at Jack's Animal Rescue, we believe in quality over quantity. It's a known fact that Manitoba has a high population of dogs looking for loving, forever homes. We work with each & every dog in our care to provide them with the best opportunity to succeed. This includes:
- making sure they are healthy
- addressing any behavioral problems
- considering all interested applications for the best fit
- offering follow-up support for new adopters
When you adopt from Jack's Animal Rescue, you are not alone! Our team will support you every step of the way to help you find and save your new best friend.
