Is There A Phone Number I Can Call?

Due to the resuce being a volunteer service and being run out of the owener's home the only dedicated phone would be her personal phone. As a result of this the only way to contact us is via email or our facebook page.



What Makes The Rescue Different?

Compared to other resuces who care more about quanity of dogs rehomed we at JAR care more about the quality of the dogs happiness and wellbeing resulting in happier dogs and as a result overall easier and happier rehoming.



How Do I Support The Resuce?

First of all we want to thank you for even considering helping the rescue! You can support us by dontating animal food which can be open, towels, sheets blankets, soap and dryer sheets, kennels (wire or plastics), or with a monetary donation. This along with just volunteering at the rescue.



Are The Animals At the Rescue Healthy?

Yes, all the animals at the rescue are seen by a vet monthly this along with all dogs being fixed, tatooed, and checked for worms.
